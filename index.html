About

I am a third year PhD student of Information and Communication Engineering, in Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), supervised by Professor Honggang Zhang and Chun-guang Li. And I am also a member of the Pattern Recognition and Intelligent Systems (PRIS) laboratory at BUPT. Previously, I received my B. E. from Jilin University in 2012. The detailed biography will be found in my Curriculum Vitae.

My research interests include computer vision, pattern recognition, and machine learning. Recently, I focused my research on the following topics:

  • Person re-identication and Image/Video annotation,
  • Metric learning and Multi-view learning,
  • Deep learning and Zero-shot learning.
